Why this matters
Built around real Massachusetts data
Every design decision is grounded in what the data says about who needs help and why they aren't getting it.
🏪
Most businesses never find what they qualify for
The top reason businesses don't access state programs is not knowing they exist. MassConnect solves this by proactively surfacing relevant programs based on a business's actual profile.
⚖️
Access is determined by who you know
68% of Black and 70% of Latino business owners cite capital access as a top priority — yet these businesses are least likely to know about available programs. MassConnect levels that playing field.
👥
Staff time goes to intake, not impact
With 4,000+ annual inquiries and limited navigators, most staff time is spent on manual triage. MassConnect handles intake automatically so human attention goes to the 500 businesses that need it most.
🤖
60% of MA businesses see AI as an opportunity
The businesses most underserved by the current system — Black, Latino, and AAPI entrepreneurs — are also the most open to AI-powered tools. The timing is right.
What happens next

Three steps to advance MassConnect

This submission is a working concept — not a proposal. The AI beta is live and ready to use today. Here is the path from concept to pilot.

1
30-minute scoping call
Align on pilot scope, success metrics, and which agency to start with. MassDevelopment is the recommended first partner.
2
90-day pilot
Deploy with one agency. Validate matching accuracy, staff adoption, and business outcomes before scaling.
3
Scale statewide
Expand across all 13 agencies with a proven model. No legacy system overhauls. No additional headcount.
